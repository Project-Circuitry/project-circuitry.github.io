About Project Circuitry

Project Circuitry is a cutting-edge robotic platform designed with a singular focus: making remote presence and accessibility genuinely useful and easy to use for everyone.

Our sleek, controllable robot features an integrated camera system that allows users to remotely navigate spaces, attend events, participate in education, and maintain connections in ways previously impossible.

Whether you're a student attending class from home, someone with mobility challenges exploring new places, or an educator bringing the world into your classroom, Project Circuitry is designed for you.
